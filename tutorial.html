Tutorial

Welcome to the Digi Memo Tutorial Page!

Here you'll find resources to help you master the art of note-taking with our app. We'll cover everything from basic note creation to advanced features like reminders and search.

Getting Started

Creating a Note

  1. Navigate to the "Add a Note" section on the right side of the screen.
  2. Type your note in the text area provided.
  3. (Optional) Set a reminder date using the date picker.
  4. Click the "Add" button (the plus icon) to save your note.

Viewing Your Notes

Your notes are displayed on the right side of the screen under the "Your Notes" section. Each note is presented in a card format, displaying the note content, timestamp, and any set reminder date.

Features

Search

Use the search bar in the navigation bar to quickly find notes containing specific keywords. The search function highlights matching text within your notes.

Reminders

Set reminder dates for your notes to ensure you never miss an important deadline. Notes with upcoming due dates are highlighted, and you'll receive a notification on the day the note is due.

Edit

Click the "Edit" button (the pencil icon) on a note card to modify its content or reminder date.

Delete

Click the "Delete" button (the trash can icon) on a note card to permanently remove it.

Tips and Tricks

  • Organize with Reminders: Utilize reminder dates to categorize your notes based on priority or deadlines.
  • Use Descriptive Titles: Give your notes clear and concise titles to facilitate easy searching later.

FAQs

Q: How many notes can I create?

A: You can create up to 25 notes.

Q: Can I change the appearance of the app?

A: Currently, customization options are limited. However, you can use the search and reminder features to personalize your note-taking experience.

Q: Can I access my notes from other devices?

A: As of now, notes are stored locally on your device. We are exploring options for cloud synchronization in the future.
